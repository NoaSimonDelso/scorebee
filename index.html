How has your MEP voted on interests you care about?

The for the European Parliament are going to take place across Europe between 22 and 25 May. We’re making it easier for you to find out how politicians in Brussels voted on issues that affect Europe’s future:

Which parties have supported action on climate change? How did your representative in the EU vote on fracking? Who wants GMOs and who doesn’t?

ScoreEP puts a number, out of 100, on your representative’s support or opposition to these issues.
